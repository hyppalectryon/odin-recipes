Lasagna

What is Lasagna, anyway???

Lasagna is a baked Italian dish consisting of wide strips of pasta cooked and layered with meat or vegetables, cheese, and tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Spices
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

How do I make my own?

  1. Make meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the different layers.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let lasagna rest before serving.
